about me.

Hello! I'm Kelly Lindsay, a web developer passionate about creating beautiful and functional digital interfaces.

I discovered my love for coding during the pandemic, where I customised small sections of code for those who were desperate to get their small businesses online. As I worked on the backend, I realized how much I enjoyed the challenge and made the decision to pursue a career in web development.

I've worked hard to hone my skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and most recently, I've branched out into new languages, including React, C# and Java.

In my free time, I develop mobile games with my partner, with our beloved cat alongside us.
If you're interested in working together, please get in touch!
